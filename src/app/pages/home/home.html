Recipick

Your AI Fridge Companion

Why Choose Recipick?

Recipick flips the logic of cooking on its head. Instead of searching for a recipe and then shopping for ingredients, look at what you have on hand and create a recipe. It's a real solution for saving money and reducing food waste at home. Our "Pick" feature is powered by context-aware AI. If a user only has "Eggs and Soy Sauce," Recipick won't suggest "Fried Rice" (because the user doesn't have rice), but instead will suggest "Pontianak Fried Egg" which specifically uses those ingredients. With a design inspired by a physical recipe book and a fun font, users will feel like they're consulting with a friendly "Cooking Friend" or "Kitchen Assistant." This app is designed to relieve stress when you're unsure what to cook. Because it's based on Generative AI, Recipick's recipes are limitless. Users can enjoy a unique Indonesian menu every day, providing an element of surprise every time they press the "Pick!" button.

How Recipick Works?

First step, take a look at what you have today. Is there an egg? Half a bunch of kale? Or just leftover rice from last night?


Action

Enter the name of the ingredient into the app's search bar.


Tips

The more specific the ingredient, the more accurate the AI's "picks" will be.

Once all the ingredients are listed, it's time for the magic to begin.


Action

Click the "Pick My Recipe" button (the Recipe Book icon with the AI ​​glow).


Process

Behind the scenes, Gemini AI will scan thousands of possible Indonesian recipes and choose the one that best suits your ingredients.

In seconds, a personalized recipe will appear on your screen.


Action

Follow the simplified cooking steps.


Visual

You'll see the estimated time, difficulty level, and instructions neatly laid out like notes in your favorite cookbook.

Pro Tips for Maximum Results


Secret Ingredients: Have a secret spice like shrimp paste or oyster sauce? Add it in! The AI will try to incorporate it to enhance the flavor.


Save Favorites: If you like the results, don't forget to save the recipe to your personal "Digital Recipe Book" so you can cook it again later.
